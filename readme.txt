== Description ==

Boomtrain is a personalized mobile and email notification platform. Track various events within your WordPress posts.

By enabling the Boomtrain Tracker on your WordPress website, you could track all "viewed" events and store them to your Boomtrack dashboard.

You can also add a Signup Form snippet embedded on your page to increase your lead generation and get more subscribers for your service. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload `boomtrain-tracker/` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Navigate to the 'Boomtrain' admin dashboard menu
1. Copy your API snippet from your Boomtrain profile at the `Boomtrain Snippet` textarea
